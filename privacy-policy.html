Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2025

Welcome to Energeia. Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use our energy drink tracking app.

Information We Collect

Energeia does not collect or transmit any personal information to external servers. All data you enter (e.g., energy drink consumption, caffeine and sugar intake) is stored locally on your device.

We do not:

  • Require you to create an account
  • Access or store your email, IP address, or location
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Share your data with third parties

Data Security

Since all data is stored on your device, its security depends on your device's own protections (e.g., passcode, encryption). We do not have access to your data and cannot recover it if deleted.

Your Rights

You are in full control of your data. You may delete the app at any time to remove all stored information.

Children's Privacy

Energeia is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. The latest version will always be available within the app or via our website.

Contact Us

If you have questions, email us at:

  • privacy@energeia.app
